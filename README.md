# 🚀 Doable
### *The Missing Layer Between AI and Execution*

> **Built for the Not Your College (NYC) CodeQuest Hackathon 2026**

---

## 🌍 Overview

Every day, millions of people ask AI questions like:

- *How do I learn Web Development?*
- *How do I prepare for a Hackathon?*
- *How do I crack interviews?*
- *How do I stay consistent?*

AI provides excellent answers.

Yet, most users never take the first step.

The problem isn't finding information anymore.

The problem is **executing it consistently.**

That observation led us to build **Doable**.

---

# ❌ The Problem

Modern AI assistants are incredible at generating knowledge.

They can explain concepts, generate roadmaps, and answer almost anything.

However, they all stop at the same place:

> **The answer.**

Once the conversation ends,

there is no accountability,
no structured execution,
no progress tracking,
and no motivation to continue.

Users often:

- Read the response
- Bookmark it
- Promise to start tomorrow
- Forget about it

Days later...

they ask the exact same question again.

The gap between **knowing** and **doing** is what inspired Doable.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

## Doable

### **AI + Planner + Accountability**

Instead of acting like another chatbot,

Doable transforms every goal into a guided journey.

Whether your goal is:

- Learn React
- Participate in a Hackathon
- Build a Portfolio
- Crack Interviews
- Learn DSA

Doable doesn't just explain it.

It helps you finish it.

---

# ⚙️ How Doable Works

### 1️⃣ Describe your goal

Example:

> "I want to participate in a Hackathon."

---

### 2️⃣ AI understands your objective

The AI generates:

- A clear explanation
- A structured roadmap
- Milestones
- Small actionable tasks

---

### 3️⃣ Focus on one task

Instead of overwhelming users with dozens of steps,

Doable unlocks progress one task at a time.

---

### 4️⃣ Submit proof

After completing a task,

users upload proof of completion.

(Currently demonstrated as an MVP.)

---

### 5️⃣ Continue the journey

As users progress,

they move closer to completing their goal.

---

### 6️⃣ Build your world

Instead of points or meaningless badges,

every completed task contributes to building a personalized virtual world.

It serves as a visual reminder that consistency creates progress.
